Background info for Oct 9th EOGM

At the EOGM, Residents will be asked to take decisions which could affect the quality of their residency at Bullion. This website provides background information designed to assist them when they vote at the EOGM. It is recommended that all residents should vote based on their own knowledge of the subject, info obtained on this website and from other sources, and based on their own needs and conscience, but please realise that there are consequences, whichever way you vote. The appealant has rights, but so have the occupants of the 14th floor. Whatever the result of the proposed resolution(s), the problem may not end there unless the core problems at the MCST, some of which were identified by the STB and the High Court, are also addressed.
