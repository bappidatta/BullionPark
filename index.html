Bullion Controversy

At the EOGM, Residents will be asked to take decisions which could affect the quality of their residency at Bullion. This website provides information/background info designed to assist them when they make their decision to vote at the EOGM. All residents should vote based on their own knowledge of the subject, info obtained on this website and from other sources, and vote based on their own needs and conscience, but please realise that there are consequences, whichever way you vote. The appealant has rights, but so have the occupants of the 14th floor. Whatever the result of the proposed resolution(s), it may not end there. More importantly, residents must ensure that the problems at the MCST, some of which are identified by the STB and the High Court, are addressed.
